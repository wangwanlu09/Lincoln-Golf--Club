Lincoln Golf Club Function Centre

PRIVATE FUNCTIONS

Lincoln Golf Club could be the perfect venue for your next private function. Whether it is an anniversary, birthday, corporate event, business house golf function, wedding or any social occassion. We can arrange for bar staff, set up and clean up to make your event a special one. Our clubrooms are well appointed and offer a great space for small groups through to a large gathering of approximately 90 people. Our hireage rates are dependant on your requirements so please contact us today to make an inquiry.
